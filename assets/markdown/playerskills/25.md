**Outside Curler (Futbolchi Skili)**

**Ta'rif:**
Outside Curler – futbolchi tashqi oyoq (strong foot ning tashqi tomoni) bilan egri zarba (trivela) va pas berish texnikasi. Weak foot zaif bo'lgan o'yinchilar uchun strong foot bilan ikki tomonga curl qilish imkonini beradi, aniqlik va masofani oshiradi. **Yaxshi statistika: Curl 85+, Finishing 80+, Weak Foot Accuracy past (Rarely/Occasionally)** (ideal 90+ Curl va low weak foot).

**Qachon va qayerda ishlatiladi:**
* Winglarda (LWF/RWF) cut inside dan keyin egri zarba/pas berishda.
* Jarima maydoni yaqinida GK ni aldash yoki 1v1'larda.
* Kontr-hujum yoki build-up'da trivela pas/shots; one-footed wingers/ST uchun ideal.

**Bajarish tartibi (oddiy tavsif):**
1. O'yinchi strong foot tomonga (masalan, right-footed LWF) joylashib, to'pni oldinga oling.
2. **Finesse shot** bajaring: Shoot bosing va LS ni egri tomonga (far post ga) burang.
3. Power 40-60% to'ldiring – tashqi oyoq bilan curl chiqadi (avtomatik trigger).
4. Pas uchun: Pass + LS egri.

**Mobile Classic Control:**
- **Finesse Shot** (R1/Curl + Shoot) 40-60% power, LS far post ga – trivela trigger.

**Hususiyatlari:**
* Strong foot bilan weak foot curl imkoniyati, aniqlik oshadi.
* Uzoq masofada ham ishlaydi (shots/passes).
* Inverted wingers uchun OP, GK ni aldashda samarali.

**Maslahatlar:**
* Weak foot "Rarely" bo'lgan one-footed wingers ga qo'shing (Vini, Di Maria).
* Long-range Curler yoki Rabona bilan combo qiling.
* Finesse low power, close range'da bajaring.
* Eng yaxshi o'yinchilar: Vinícius Jr, Di María, Quaresma, Messi (right side), Beckham.
