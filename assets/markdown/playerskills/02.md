# Double Touch (Futbolchi Skili)

**Double Touch** - eFootball'da eng kuchli va ko'p ishlatiladigan dribbling skilllaridan biri. O'yinchi to'pga tez ikki marta teginib, himoyachidan qochadi, bo'sh joy ochadi yoki shot/pass ga o'tadi. 2026 versiyasida first touch va counter-attack uchun **S-tier OP skill**, chunki tezlik saqlanadi va aniq boshqaruv beradi.

## Tarifi
O'yinchi to'pni ikki tez teginish bilan boshqaradi, yo'nalish o'zgartiradi yoki himoyani aldab qoldiradi. **Double Touch** badge'iga bog'liq (Skill Token bilan qo'shiladi). Faqat badge'li o'yinchilar bajara oladi, animatsiya silliq va tez.

## Qachon va Qayerda Ishlatilishi
- **1v1 duel** va himoyachi yaqinlashganda driblingda.
- **First touch** sifatida pass qabul qilganda (tez counter).
- **Penalty box** yaqinida shot angle ochish yoki foul tortish.
- **Tight spaces** va multiple pressure'da bo'sh joy yaratish.
- **Wing**'larda cut inside yoki cross oldidan.
- **Midfield**'da possession saqlash va switch.
**Eslatma:** Juda sekin yoki orqa tomonda ishlatmang – tezlik yo'qotiladi.

## Bajarish Tartibi (Mobile Classic Control)
**Asosiy input:** **Dash Tap + LS Flick** (2026 da eng consistent).
1. **Dribling** qiling yoki **pass qabul** qilayotgan bo'ling (to'p yaqinida).
2. **Dash** tugmasini **tez bosing va darhol bo'sating** (tap & release).
3. **Darhol** (0.1 soniya ichida) **LS** ni **oldinga yoki diagonal yo'nalishga tez flick** qiling:
   - **Oldinga:** Tezlik saqlash.
   - **Diagonal:** Cut inside yoki burilish.
   - **Yon/Orqaga:** Himoyadan qochish.
4. O'yinchi **ikki teginish** bajarib, to'pni boshqaradi.
**Variantlar:**
- **Hold Dash** + flick (ba'zi o'yinchilarda ishlaydi, lekin tap yaxshiroq).
- **Smart Assist** yoqilsa: Osonroq.
- **Chain:** Keyin Shoot yoki Sprint.
**Training:** Academy > Command List > Dribbling > Double Touch. 200 marta mashq.

## Hususiyatlari
- **Tez va aniq:** Himoyachini aldab, separation yaratadi.
- **Universal:** First touch, dribble, shot ga o'tish.
- **Speed saqlaydi:** 2026 da acceleration + balance tiklanadi.
- **Flair + Utility:** Foul tortadi, GK ni aldash.
**Kamchiliklari:** Faqat badge'li o'yinchilar, timing qiyin, spam o'qiladi.

## Maslahatlar
- **Timing:** Contact oldidan bajaring, delay – fail.
- **Mashq:** Free Training'da pass + Double Touch + Shoot combo.
- **Combo'lar:**
  | Combo | Natija |
  |-------|--------|
  | Double Touch + Shoot | Gol mashinasi (stunner). |
  | Double Touch + Scissors | 1v1 killer. |
  | Double Touch + Sprint | Counter tezlik. |
- **Player:** Dribbling 92+, Agility, Balance yuqori.
- **Spam yo'q:** Mix pass/shot bilan unpredictable.
- **Settings:** Sensitivity high, Analogue Sprint on.
- **2026 Pro:** First touch da OP – Division 1 uchun must.
