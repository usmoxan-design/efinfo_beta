**Pinpoint Crossing (Futbolchi Skili)**

**Ta'rif:**
Pinpoint Crossing – futbolchi jarima maydoni ichiga egri va aniq krosslar (crosses) berish texnikasi. Krosslarning aniqligini va curl (burilish) ni oshiradi, "great cross" chastotasini ko'paytiradi. **Yaxshi statistika: Lofted Pass 85+, Curl 85+, Kicking Power 85+** (ideal 90+ Lofted Pass va Curl).

**Qachon va qayerda ishlatiladi:**
* Winglarda (LWF/RWF) yoki fullback'larda (LB/RB) jarima maydoni yaqinidan kross berishda.
* Cross taktikalarida (out wide), kontr-hujumlarda yoki set-piece'larda.
* Himoyani buzib, CF/ST ga aniq to'p yetkazishda; Cross Specialist playstyle bilan OP.

**Bajarish tartibi (oddiy tavsif):**
1. Wingda dribling qilib, jarima maydoni yaqiniga boring.
2. **Cross** bajaring: Pass tugmasini **L1 + Square (PS) / LB + X (Xbox)** bosing (lofted cross).
3. Power gauge 50-70% to'ldiring, receiver'ni tanlang (far post uchun ideal).
4. Avtomatik curl va aniq traektoriya bilan kross o'tadi.

**Mobile Classic Control:**
- **Lofted Cross** tugmasini bosing (LS bilan yo'nalish), power 50-70% – pinpoint trigger.

**Hususiyatlari:**
* Kross aniqligi +10-20%, egri traektoriya (curl) qo'shadi.
* Past traektoriyali tez krosslar, GK ni aldashda samarali.
* LB/RB/Winger uchun must-have, ammo stats past bo'lsa zaif.

**Maslahatlar:**
* High Lofted Pass/Curl'li o'yinchilarga qo'shing, Weighted Pass bilan combo qiling.
* Far post ga nishonlang, receiver Heading'li bo'lsin.
* Edged Crossing bilan birgalikda OP (Theo Hernández kabi).
* Eng yaxshi o'yinchilar: Theo Hernández, Trent Alexander-Arnold, Dimarco, Pedri, Robertson, Beckham.
