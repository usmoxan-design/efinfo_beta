**Low Lofted Pass (Futbolchi Skili)**

**Ta'rif:**
Low Lofted Pass – futbolchi uzoq masofali lofted pass'larni past traektoriya bilan (past uchuvchi, tez yetib boruvchi) aniq berish texnikasi. Oddiy lofted pass'larga nisbatan pastroq va tezroq bo'lib, qabul qiluvchi yugurib ketayotganda oson nazorat qilish imkonini beradi. **Yaxshi statistika: Lofted Pass 80+, Low Pass 82+** (ideal 90+ Lofted Pass va 85+ Low Pass).

**Qachon va qayerda ishlatiladi:**
* Uzoq lofted pass va lofted through ball'larda (switch play, kontr-hujum).
* Wing yoki fullback'larda diagonal pas berishda.
* LB/RB/AMF/CMF pozitsiyalarida, himoyani tez o'tib o'tishda; running receiver'lar uchun ideal.

**Bajarish tartibi (oddiy tavsif):**
1. Uzoq masofaga lofted pass yoki through ball bajaring (avtomatik trigger).
2. Lofted Pass tugmasini bosing (L1 + Pass/Through).
3. Power 50-70% to'ldiring – past traektoriya bilan tez yetib boradi.

**Mobile Classic Control:**
- **Lofted Through** yoki **Lofted Pass** tugmasini bosing – skill bilan past va tez bo'ladi (stunning pass bilan ham).

**Hususiyatlari:**
* Past traektoriya: Receiver tez nazorat qiladi, interception kamayadi.
* Lofted through ball va switch play'da samarali.
* Weighted Pass bilan combo OP (backspin + past).
* Fullback/Winger uchun must-have.

**Maslahatlar:**
* Yuqori Lofted Pass'li o'yinchilarga qo'shing, Pinpoint Crossing yoki Through Passing bilan combo qiling.
* Running winger'ga diagonal pas uchun ishlatmang.
* Training'da mashq qiling – situational trigger.
* Eng yaxshi o'yinchilar: Beckham, Theo Hernández, Trent AA, De Bruyne, Pirlo (yuqori lofted pass'li).
