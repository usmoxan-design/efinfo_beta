**Weighted Pass (Futbolchi Skili)**

**Ta'rif:**
Weighted Pass – futbolchi lofted pass va through ball'larga backspin qo'shib, aniqlikni oshirish texnikasi. To'pni to'g'ri to'xtatadi, ortib ketishini oldini oladi. **Yaxshi statistika: Low Pass 82+, Lofted Pass 80+** (ideal 90+ Low Pass va Lofted Pass).

**Qachon va qayerda ishlatiladi:**
* Lofted through pass va long lofted pass'larda (o'z yarim maydoningizdan).
* Kontr-hujum yoki possession'da himoyani buzish uchun.
* AMF/CMF/SS/Winger/DMF/CB pozitsiyalarida, final third oldidan.

**Bajarish tartibi (oddiy tavsif):**
1. Hamkasbni ochiq joyga yuboring.
2. **Lofted Through Pass** bajaring: L1 + Through Pass (mobile: Lofted Through tugmasi).
3. Power 40-70% to'ldiring, backspin avtomatik qo'shiladi.
4. To'p aniq to'xtaydi.

**Mobile Classic Control:**
- **Lofted Through** tugmasini bosing, LS bilan yo'nalishni aniqlang – skill trigger.

**Hususiyatlari:**
* Backspin qo'shib, to'pni to'xtatadi (ortib ketmaydi).
* Lofted pass/through ball aniqligi oshadi.
* Long distance'da samarali, cross emas.

**Maslahatlar:**
* Ground/low through pass'da ishlamaydi, faqat lofted.
* Through Pass yoki Low Lofted Pass bilan combo qiling.
* 82+ Low Pass'li o'yinchilarga qo'shing – max effekt.
* Eng yaxshi o'yinchilar: Messi, Mbappé, Cruyff, Maradona, Foden, Neymar (default'li), De Bruyne.
