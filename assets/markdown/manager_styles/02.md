eFootball Mobile (hozirgi eFootball 2026 Mobile) da menejerning **Quick Counter** (Tez qarshi hujum) uslubi haqida to'liq va tushunarli izoh beraman. Bu uslub haqiqiy futbolning Mourinho (Inter) yoki Simeone (Atletico) uslubiga o'xshaydi: chuqur himoya qiling, to'pni qaytarib oling va darhol tez paslar/through ball bilan hujumni boshlang. Raqibni charchatish o'rniga, xatolardan foydalanib, chaqmoqdek zarba bering. Possessiondan farqli o'laroq, shoshilinch va tezlik asosiy.

### Quick Counter qanday ishlaydi? (O'yinchi harakatlari)
Konami ta'rifiga ko'ra:
- **Himoyada**: Chuqur chiziq (deep line), kompakt turish. Butun jamoa press qilmaydi – faqat yaqin o'yinchilar bosim qiladi, qolganlari joyida turadi.
- **Hujumda**: To'pni qaytarganda, hujumchilar darhol oldinga yuguradi (dash). Through ball, lofted pass bilan qanotlar va markazga uzating. Qisqa pas emas – tezlik muhim.
- **To'pni qaytarib olganda**: Hujumchilar (CF, Wingers) darhol sprint qiladi, midfield pas variantlari beradi.
- **Zaif tomoni**: Yuqori pressdan qo'rqmang, lekin possession jamoalarga qarshi sabr kerak – deep himoya saqlang.

Natija: Kam possession (30-40%), lekin ko'p gol. Div1 da LBC dominant, lekin QC Rijkaard bilan top10 da bor.

Individual instructions: FB larni defensive qiling (playstyle o'chirish), CF lar counter target.

### Jamoa qurish: Eng yaxshi o'yinchilar
**Speed (tezlik), Finishing, Through Passing, Stamina** prioritet. Playstyles:
- **GK**: Tall + Offensive (Courtois, Neuer) – tez chiqib keladi.
- **CB**: Destroyer/Build Up + tez (Koulibaly, Timber, Araujo, Van Dijk) – deep line uchun sprint kerak!
- **FB**: Defensive/Attacking FB lekin instructions defensive (Tomiyasu, Di Lorenzo, Cancelo).
- **DMF**: Anchor Man (Zakaria, Rodri) – himoyani ushlab turadi.
- **CMF**: Orchestrator/Box-to-Box (Pedri, Kimmich, Bellingham).
- **AMF/SS**: Creative Playmaker/Hole Player (Neymar, De Bruyne, Rafa Silva).
- **Wingers**: Prolific/Roaming Flank (Son, Mbappe LWF, Saka, Yamal, Vini Jr).
- **CF**: Goal Poacher (Mbappe, Haaland, Alvarez).

**F2P**: POTW Son, Gakpo, Van Dijk. Training: Speed/Dribbling/Finishing.

### O'yin maslahatlari (Tips)
1. **Himoyada sabr**: Deep turib, raqibni oldinga torting. Manual press shoshmang – AI press ishlat.
2. **Hujumda**: Through/lofted pass bering (L1 + pas). Mini-mapdan yuguruvchilarni ko'ring, offside ehtiyot.
3. **Counter**: To'pni qaytarganda darhol forward – 2-3 pasda gol.
4. **Zaiflik**: Possessionga qarshi deep line, LBC ga qarshi yuqori press.
5. **Fitness**: Stamina 90+ bo'lsin, sub tez.
6. **Div1 uchun**: Rijkaard + 4-2-2-2 bilan 70% win. Formani sinab ko'ring.
