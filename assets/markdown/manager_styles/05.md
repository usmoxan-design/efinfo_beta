eFootball Mobile (hozirgi eFootball 2026 Mobile) da menejerning **Long Ball** (Uzun to'p) uslubi haqida to'liq va tushunarli izoh beraman. Bu uslub haqiqiy futbolning klassik "route one" ingliz uslubi yoki Franz Beckenbauerning Bayern Munich taktikalariga o'xshaydi: to'pni himoyadan to'g'ridan-to'g'ri target striker (fokal hujumchi) ga uzun lofted pass bilan uzating, ikkinchi to'plarni (second balls) oling va tez gol uring. Long Ball Counter (LBC) dan farqli o'laroq, bu ko'proq possessionga asoslangan (50-60%): to'pni biroz ushlang, oldinga yuguruvchilarni kuting, keyin uzun to'p bering. LBC esa chuqur himoya + darhol counter. Hozirgi metada LBC dominant, lekin Long Ball F2P va qulay – Beckenbauer bilan Div1 top50 da bor.

### Long Ball qanday ishlaydi? (O'yinchi harakatlari)
Konami ta'rifiga ko'ra: himoyachilar va o'rta sahnadan target man ga tez uzun pas, qo'llab-quvvatlovchilar second balls uchun.
- **Hujumda**: Himoya va midfield target striker/WB ga uzun lofted pass izlaydi. Hujumchilar bir-biriga yaqin turib, second balls ni oladi. Possession bilan build-up, keyin vertical long ball.
- **Himoyada**: Chuqurroq chiziq (deep-mid block), kompakt – aerial duellarga tayyor. Konservativ press.
- **To'pni qaytarib olganda**: Darhol uzun pas striker ga yoki wide outlets ga – midfield second balls uchun.
- **To'pni yo'qotganda**: Tez kompakt blokga qayting, press emas – structure saqlang.
Zaif tomoni: Yuqori pressdan qo'rqmang, lekin quick counterdan ehtiyot (tez CB kerak). Natija: Territory tez oling, bypass press.

### Jamoa qurish: Eng yaxshi o'yinchilar

- **FB/WB**: Defensive/Attacking FB (Cancelo, Davies – stamina yuqori).

### O'yin maslahatlari (Tips)
1. **Uzun paslar**: L1 + triangle (lofted long) – target man ga, mini-mapdan yuguruvchilarni ko'ring. Offside ehtiyot!
2. **Hujumda**: Possession bilan build-up, keyin long ball. Second balls ni olib finish.
3. **Himoyada**: Deep-mid block saqlang, aerial duel yuting. Manual intercept.
4. **Zaiflik**: QC ga qarshi deep line, possessionga qarshi sub LBC ga o'ting.
5. **Fitness**: Stamina 90+, sub target o'xshash.
6. **Div1 uchun**: Beckenbauer 3-2-2-3 bilan 65% win. Link-up faollashtiring (Orchestrator DMF + Poacher CF).
