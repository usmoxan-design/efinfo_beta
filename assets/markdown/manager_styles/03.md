eFootball Mobile (hozirgi eFootball 2026 Mobile) da menejerning **Long Ball Counter** (LBC, Uzun to'p qarshi hujum) uslubi haqida to'liq va tushunarli izoh beraman. Bu uslub haqiqiy futbolning Sean Dyche (Everton) yoki klassik ingliz "route one" taktikalariga o'xshaydi: chuqur himoya qiling, to'pni qaytarib oling va darhol uzun lofted passlar (yuqori uzun paslar) bilan hujumchilarga uzating. Quick Counterdan farqli o'laroq, qisqa through ball emas – to'g'ridan-to'g'ri CB/GK dan CF ga uzun to'p. Raqibni charchatish o'rniga, xatolardan foydalanib, bir necha pasda gol uring. Hozirgi meta (Div1 top10) da dominant – 80%+ o'yinchilar LBC ishlatadi.

### Long Ball Counter qanday ishlaydi? (O'yinchi harakatlari)
Konami ta'rifiga ko'ra: hujumchilar orqaga yugurib, himoya chizig'i orqasida joy ochadi; himoyachilar uzun pas variantlari beradi.
- **Himoyada**: Eng chuqur chiziq (deep block), kompakt turish. Press faqat yaqin o'yinchilar – butun jamoa yuqoridan bosmaydi.
- **Hujumda**: To'p qaytarganda, CF/wingers darhol sprint qiladi. Lofted pass/uzun to'p bilan uzating – midfieldni o'tkazib yuboring. Hujumchilar to'pni ushlab, ikkinchi to'pni kutadi.
- **To'pni qaytarib olganda**: Hujumchilar oldinga yuguradi, CB/GK lofted pass beradi.
- **Zaif tomoni**: Possession jamoalarga qarshi possession kam (30-40%), lekin gol ko'p. Yuqori pressdan xavfsizroq.

Natija: Kam possession, lekin yuqori gol samaradorligi. Div1 da Capello bilan top1-5.

### Jamoa qurish: Eng yaxshi o'yinchilar
**Lofted Pass (yuqori pas), Long Pass, Speed, Finishing, Aerial Ability, Heading, Stamina** prioritet. Playstyles:
- **GK**: Offensive GK (uzun pas beradi). Donnarumma, Courtois.
- **CB**: Build Up/Destroyer + tall (Van Dijk, Araujo, Saliba, Kim Min-jae) – uzun pas chiqaradi!
- **FB**: Defensive FB (Carvajal, Kounde).
- **DMF**: Anchor Man (Rodri, Tchouameni) – deep himoya.
- **CMF**: Box-to-Box/Orchestrator (Kimmich, Rice, Onana).
- **AMF/SS**: Hole Player/Creative Playmaker (De Bruyne, Musiala).
- **Wingers**: Prolific Winger/Roaming Flank (Vinicius, Yamal, Dembele) – tez yugursin.
- **CF**: Goal Poacher/Fox in Box + tall (Haaland, Kane, Lukaku, Isak).

**F2P**: POTW Haaland, Van Dijk, Rodri. Training: Lofted Pass/Speed/Heading.

### O'yin maslahatlari (Tips)
1. **Uzun paslar**: L1 + triangle (lofted through) – mini-mapdan yuguruvchilarni ko'ring, offside ehtiyot.
2. **Himoyada**: Deep block saqlang, manual intercept qiling. Raqib possession bo'lsa, sabr qiling.
3. **Hujumda**: CB dan darhol CF ga – 1-2 pasda gol. Ikkinchi to'pni olib, finish.
4. **Zaiflik**: Yuqori pressdan xavfsiz, lekin possessionga qarshi sub LBC dan Possessionga o'ting.
5. **Fitness**: Stamina 90+, sub tez. Link-up Over the Top tanlang.
6. **Div1 uchun**: Capello + 4-2-2-2 bilan 75%+ win. Forma sinab ko'ring.
